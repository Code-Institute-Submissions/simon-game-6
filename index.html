Simon Game

Instructions


This Simon game is a memory game where the computer will flash a colour and you have to click on the correct colour to move on to the next level. They are two modes, medium and hard. (See below for instructions on each mode.)

Medium


In this mode you get 2 lives before game over. Each level will carry on the sequence from the level before. When the coloured button flashes, click on the correct coloured button to move on. As the levels get higher, the game gets harder.

Hard


For the hard mode you only have 1 life before it's game over. Like the medium mode, you must copy the sequence to move on to the next level. However for this mode, the coloured buttons will change positions. When the coloured buttons change positions you must follow and click on the correct button to move on to the next level!
